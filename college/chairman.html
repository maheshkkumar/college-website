Chairman's Message




The Oxford Group of Educational Institutions established in the year 1974 under the aegis of Children’s Education Society (R) and has proved a beacon to the knowledge seeker not only all over the country but also all over the world. CES made a humble beginning with 24 students and 2 teachers. Today, with a total dedication and commitment from the Management and sincere involvement of the Head of the Institutions and the faculty members, The Oxford Group of Institutions numbering 32 have more than 15,000 students and 2,500 teaching staff with more than 85 academic streams from primary level to Doctoral level in the academic fields of Management, Hospitality, Science, Engineering, Dental and Medical Courses.
The Oxford College of Engineering (TOCE) is one of the most prestigious Institutions under The Oxford Educational Institutions and it provides quality training in professional education in various streams of Engineering, M.Tech, Post Graduate programmes in Computer Application (MCA) and in Business Administration (MBA) along with active research in all the departments.

The college campus is set in a sprawling 6.25 acres prime land on NH 7, ideal for education in a serene environment which is also known as the IT Corridor in Bangalore, with over 65,000 Sq.mt of built-up area spread over two buildings for academic purposes. The college has a dedicated and highly qualified team of faculty who offer Quality education to the students as per the academic scheme of Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), Belgaum. The Oxford College of Engineering is equipped with the state of the art laboratory infrastructure and strong Teaching Learning process which includes tutorials, remedial classes, expert lectures, students’ centric workshops and seminars. One of our deep concerns is that the quality of the technical education imparted demands well developed course contents, e-contents and exposure to virtual labs and access to pedagogical practices. With our excellent infrastructure facilities like blade/rack servers and around 1600 desktops, a dedicated 12 Mbps leased line internet connectivity, smart class rooms equipped with LCD projectors, audio and Wi-Fi internet facilities, video conferencing board rooms, the college offers the best infrastructure to its students.

The performance of TOCE in academic front has been consistently on the rise and it is evident from the fact that the college has been bagging several ranks in the term end examinations of VTU. The placement scenario is also quite encouraging even during the period of recession and decreased hiring from mass recruiters.

TOCE is recipient of several research projects from various funding agencies like DRDO, AICTE, BRNS, NIF, VGST and VTU. These projects add value to the intellectual property of the institute and also provide an unique opportunity to its students to work on live projects of national importance. Overall it is a bundle of best practices in higher education and exposure to latest cutting edge technology, that is being offered to the students of this institute.

Sri. S. NARASARAJU
Chairman
